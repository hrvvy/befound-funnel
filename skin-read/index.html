Before you book

Questions, answered straight.

Do I need to take the Phase Check first?

No. The scan reads everything we need on the day. If you're curious where your barrier stands before coming in, the Phase Check takes 90 seconds and gives us useful context to start from.

Is a Skin Read a facial?

No. Nothing is applied to your skin and nothing is extracted. It's a measurement and a conversation: your barrier scanned at machine level, then explained, then mapped to a path.

What happens after the 45 minutes?

You take your Findings home. If a program fits your skin, we'll tell you once, plainly, and the decision is yours to make in your own time. There's no follow-up pressure.

My skin is sensitive and reactive. Is this safe for me?

Sensitive, reactive skin is exactly the skin we read most. Nothing touches your skin during the Read except the scan, and the whole point is finding out why your skin reacts the way it does.

What should I bring?

Just yourself. If you want to make the conversation sharper, bring the products you're currently using, or photos of them. Bare skin scans best, so come fresh-faced if you can.

I've already tried everything. How is this different?

Everything you've tried so far was chosen by guessing: by eye, by review, by recommendation. The Skin Read replaces the guessing with a measurement. Once you know exactly where your barrier stands, what to do next stops being a gamble.
